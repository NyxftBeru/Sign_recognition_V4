About SignEase

SignEase is an AI-powered web application designed to break communication barriers by enabling real-time sign language recognition using your webcam. Our mission is to make sign language more accessible and bridge the gap between the hearing and deaf communities.

Key Features

  • Real-time sign language detection using advanced hand tracking technology
  • Easy-to-use, modern, and responsive interface
  • Cross-platform support for all modern browsers
  • Privacy-focused: all processing happens in your browser

Our Vision

We believe in a world where technology empowers everyone to communicate freely. SignEase is a step towards making sign language recognition accessible to all, fostering inclusivity and understanding.
